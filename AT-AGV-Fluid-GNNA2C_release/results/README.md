# Released results

`metrics/` contains the official three-seed, 1000-episode summaries and the
unified deterministic comparison of Uniform, Demand-driven, and GNN-A2C.
`tables/` is regenerated by `scripts/reproduce_tables.py`.

The three files in `checkpoints/` are the final Episode-1000 checkpoints for
seeds 20260801, 20260811, and 20260821. No Episode-50, Episode-200, `latest.pt`,
temporary, failed-run, or unpublished experiment checkpoint is included.

The released numeric results are fixed research artifacts. Re-training can
produce small differences because of software, hardware, and random-number
implementation details.
